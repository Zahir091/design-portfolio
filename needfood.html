Need Food

A mobile app connecting individuals with local food banks and volunteer opportunities.

View Figma Design

Project Overview

Food insecurity remains a pressing issue in New York City, leaving many without reliable access to nutritious meals. Need Food is a mobile app designed to bridge this gap by connecting individuals with local food banks, offering food ordering and delivery services, and providing volunteer opportunities.

Key Features

  • Find and request food from nearby food banks via pickup or delivery
  • Sign up for volunteer opportunities at local organizations
  • Stay updated on food bank events and community initiatives
  • Donate directly to support food assistance programs

Research & Insights

Understanding the Problem

To design a solution that truly addressed food insecurity, we explored:

  • The challenges people face in accessing food banks (e.g., transportation, limited hours, stigma).
  • How food banks manage resources and serve their communities.
  • The role of volunteers and donors in sustaining food assistance programs.

Key Takeaways

  • Many individuals rely on food banks but struggle with transportation, making delivery an essential feature.
  • Volunteers play a crucial role, but there wasn't a simple way to sign up for opportunities.
  • Users wanted a way to track received food and plan meals based on available resources.

Solution

✅ Making Food More Accessible

  • • Users can locate food banks through a map or list view and filter based on location, services, and hours.
  • • Pickup & delivery options ensure that those with transportation barriers can still receive food.
  • • A dietary preferences feature helps users receive food that meets their needs.

🤝 Strengthening Volunteer & Community Involvement

  • • A built-in volunteer hub makes it easy to find and sign up for opportunities.
  • • A community feed allows users to stay informed about food bank events, share resources, and engage with others.

📊 Enhancing Food Management & Sustainability

  • • A food tracking tool helps users log received food, monitor consumption, and plan meals.
  • • Food banks can use this data to optimize inventory management and reduce food waste.

💙 Supporting Food Banks with Donations

  • • A secure donation feature allows users to contribute directly to food banks and food assistance programs.

Design Process

User Flows & Wireframes

We mapped out user journeys for different needs:

  • Requesting Food – Users browse local food banks, request items, and choose between pickup or delivery.
  • Volunteering – Users find and sign up for shifts at food banks.
  • Community Engagement – A social feed allows users to share updates, resources, and support each other.

UI Design & Accessibility

  • A clean, intuitive interface makes it easy for users of all backgrounds to navigate the app.
  • A map and list view help users locate food banks quickly.
  • The dietary preferences feature ensures users receive safe, suitable food options.

Features & Impact

🛒 Food Access Made Simple

  • • Users can search for nearby food banks, filter results by location and available services.
  • • Order food for pickup or delivery.

📌 Volunteer & Community Engagement

  • • Beyond just accessing food, Need Food empowers users to get involved.
  • • Sign up for volunteer opportunities and stay informed about events.

📊 Food Tracking & Dietary Preferences

  • • A food tracking tool helps users log received food items.
  • • Monitor consumption and plan meals effectively.
  • • Set dietary restrictions to ensure appropriate food options.

💙 Donations & Support

  • • A secure donation feature enables users to contribute directly to food banks.
  • • Help sustain these vital services.

Reflections & Next Steps

Lessons Learned

  • Accessibility was a major consideration, ensuring that those with limited digital literacy could still navigate the app.
  • Community engagement features helped shift the focus from just food distribution to building a support network.

Future Improvements

  • Expand features to support more cities beyond NYC.
  • Introduce AI-powered meal recommendations based on available food bank inventory.
