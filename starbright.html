Starbright Clothing

A clean, intuitive e-commerce platform designed to simplify online shopping.

View Figma Design

Project Overview

Many fashion e-commerce sites overwhelm shoppers with cluttered layouts, endless product options, and a frustrating checkout process. Starbright Clothing was designed to change that.

This e-commerce platform prioritizes a clean, intuitive shopping experience where users can easily find what they're looking for without feeling overloaded with information. By focusing on smart navigation, effortless filtering, and a simplified checkout process, Starbright Clothing makes online shopping faster, clearer, and more enjoyable.

Key Features

  • Advanced Filtering & Sorting – Users can refine results by size, color, and price without page reloads.
  • Quick Add-to-Cart – Items can be added directly from search results, reducing unnecessary navigation.
  • Streamlined Checkout – Fewer steps, clear payment options, and an easy-to-follow process minimize abandoned carts.
  • Personalized Profile Hub – Order history, saved favorites, and payment methods are conveniently stored in one place.

Identifying the Problem

Common E-Commerce Pain Points

  • Cognitive Overload – Too much information upfront makes it difficult for users to make decisions.
  • Difficult Navigation – Complex menus and excessive clicks disrupt the browsing experience.
  • Complicated Checkout – A long, confusing checkout process leads to cart abandonment.

Project Goals

  • Make shopping feel effortless, with a well-structured and intuitive layout.
  • Reduce unnecessary steps so users can browse and buy without frustration.
  • Improve product discoverability through efficient filtering and sorting options.
  • Optimize checkout speed and clarity to minimize drop-offs.

Solution

🌟 A Clean, User-Centered Shopping Experience

Instead of overwhelming users with excessive content, Starbright Clothing presents only the most relevant information at the right time.

🛍 Smart Product Discovery

  • • A refined filtering system lets users adjust search results without page reloads.
  • • Applied filters are displayed clearly below the search bar.
  • • Quick product previews give users instant access to key details.

🛒 Frictionless Add-to-Cart & Checkout

  • • Quick Add feature allows users to add items from search results.
  • • Cart pop-up confirms additions and provides an updated summary.
  • • Streamlined checkout process reduces unnecessary steps.

🎨 Thoughtful UI Design Choices

  • • Minimalist white backgrounds keep focus on products.
  • • Light blue accent color a strategic choice that conveys trust, energy, and a modern feel
  • • Subtle grey borders provide structure.
  • • Responsive hover effects enhance interactivity.

UI & Interaction Design

🖌 Color Palette & Typography

  • • White backgrounds create an open, distraction-free space.
  • • Light blue primary color draws attention to key actions.
  • • Grey borders define sections subtly.
  • • Black text ensures high contrast and readability.

🖱 Enhancing Interactivity

  • • Hover effects on buttons provide instant visual feedback.
  • • Minimal page transitions ensure smooth navigation.

Future Plans – Expanding to a Seller Marketplace

Upcoming Features for Sellers

  • Simple product listing – A hassle-free onboarding process for new sellers.
  • Inventory & order management – Easy tools to track stock levels and sales.
  • Custom storefronts – Personalized branding options for each seller.

Reflections & Next Steps

Lessons Learned

  • Reducing cognitive overload improves user confidence when browsing and purchasing.
  • Subtle design choices make a big difference in how effortless an interface feels.
  • A clearer checkout flow can significantly reduce cart abandonment rates.

Future Improvements

  • Expanding the personalization features to suggest products based on browsing history.
  • Implementing AI-powered styling recommendations for a more tailored shopping experience.
  • Enhancing mobile optimization to ensure the same seamless experience on any device.
